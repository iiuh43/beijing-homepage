Top three activities to do in Beijing

Visit the Great Wall of China

Take a day trip to hike along one of the world’s most iconic landmarks with breathtaking views.

Explore the Forbidden City

Wander through the massive palace complex that once housed emperors, filled with rich imperial history.

Stroll through the Hutongs

Discover Beijing’s old neighborhoods with narrow alleys, local snacks, traditional courtyard homes, and vibrant street life.
